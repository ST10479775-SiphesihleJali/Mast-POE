About GreenSteps Community Gardens

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Cape Town, GreenSteps began as a neighbourhood garden project...

Over the years, it has evolved into a non-profit organisation that supports over 50 community gardens nationwide.

Vision: A future where every community has access to healthy, locally grown food.
