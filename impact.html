Impact Stories

Khayelitsha Community Garden

What started as a vacant lot has become a thriving garden feeding 120 families. "Before GreenSteps, we struggled to afford fresh vegetables. Now we grow our own and even sell surplus at the local market," says garden coordinator Nomsa Mthembu.

Impact: 120 families fed, 15 permanent jobs created

Stellenbosch Primary School

The school's vegetable garden now supplies the feeding scheme with fresh produce daily. Children learn about nutrition while tending to crops, improving both their health and academic performance.

Impact: 300 students benefiting, 50% improvement in nutrition education scores

Drakensberg Rural Cooperative

In the rural mountains of KwaZulu-Natal, five villages now work together managing communal gardens. The initiative has reduced food insecurity by 70% and created a model for rural cooperation.

Impact: 5 villages connected, 200 households food secure
